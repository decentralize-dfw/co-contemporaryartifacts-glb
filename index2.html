DROP 001: CONTEMPORARY ARTIFACTS

Status co is not a concept store. It is a cultural studio producing contemporary collectibles.

A bridge between the art world and everyday rituals. These functional artifacts are designed to turn daily moments into encounters with culture.

Status co connects artists and designers to create limited editions that challenge what everyday life can look like. Each piece carries intention, imagination, and the trace of the hand.

Art is meant to be lived with, not locked away. These are not mass products, but ideas to be held.

Not merch. Not lifestyle items. A proposal for living with culture.

COLLECT IDEAS, NOT OBJECTS. USE ART DAILY.

LIVE THOUGHTFULLY.
