STATUS CO. | CONTEMPORARY ARTIFACTS

Status Co. operates as a curation platform where the distinction between daily objects and art is reimagined. Functional designs, crafted in artist studios, are released through a series of limited "drops."

The inaugural collection, "Contemporary Artifacts," presents a selection where form and function converge. In this series, vases with unconventional silhouettes, tactile rugs that transform spatial perception, and stools serving as sculptural interpretations of seating are featured. Each object is defined as a modern artifact, preserving the aesthetic language of the present for the future.

The focus extends beyond acquisition; it is placed on curating a gallery-like atmosphere within living spaces, far removed from mass production.
